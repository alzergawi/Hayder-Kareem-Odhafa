This Privacy Policy explains how RusLex handles data and content disclaimer for our users.

Our application uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data, such as your device's Advertising ID, to personalize your ad experience. We do not collect, store, or share any personal identity information on our own servers.

1.2 AI-Generated Content Disclaimer

As integrated into our main interface, all translations and linguistic explanations are generated via Artificial Intelligence. While we strive for high educational standards, AI content may contain inaccuracies. This content is provided "as is" for educational purposes.

1.3 Data Sources

Our vocabulary and linguistic database is built using processed dumps from Russian Wikipedia to ensure a comprehensive learning experience.

1.4 Permissions

The app requires internet access solely to load advertisements and download necessary database updates[cite: 12].
